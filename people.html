Modern Love is an inclusive dance party series. We've been a collective from the start. Our goal is to draw on the positivity within club culture in order to build community in New Haven (and beyond!). Beginning DJs and performers are especially encouraged to get involved. Send us a message on FB or Instagram (links in the header) and we'll get back to you.
